One quiet device, four jobs.

Gentle reminders

A soft nudge if the window passes — never an alarm, never a scold.

Caregiver notified

HOW IT WORKS

Four steps. Thirty seconds.

Tap

Phone touches the Vaxello chip on the pill case, fridge, or door frame, right where the hand already goes.

Recorded

FAQ

Questions people ask.

Does it need charging?+

No. Vaxello is passive NFC — it draws its power from the phone that taps it, so there's never a battery to manage.

Does the person need a smartphone?+

Just an NFC-enabled phone, which most modern phones already are. No app needs to be opened — the tap itself is the whole interaction.

Who sees the data?+

Only the caregiver you choose. Nothing is shared beyond that connection.

Is this a real, purchasable product?+

Vaxello is a concept design — this site is a mock-up, not a live store.
